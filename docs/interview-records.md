Interview 1
- Name: Li Wei (International Student from Malaysia)
- Date: March 26, 2026
- Background: Master's student in Shanghai, 2 years in China

Questions & Answers

Q: What challenges do you face when shopping online across borders?**
A: Currency conversion is confusing. I never know if I'm paying a fair price. Also, shipping takes forever and tracking is hard.

Q: What features would you want in a cross-border e-commerce platform?**
A: Show prices in my home currency. Clear shipping time estimates. Easy returns.

Q: How do you currently shop for international products?**
A: Taobao for local stuff, but for things from home, I use specialized apps or ask friends to bring.

Questions & Answers
