# Topic Selection: Experiment Topic 6

## Topic Name
Cross-border E-commerce Experience Platform "GlobalMart"

## Why We Chose This Topic
- E-commerce platforms have clear, well-defined functionalities
- Cross-border features (currency conversion, international shipping) are unique and challenging
- Practical relevance to international students who shop across borders
- Good balance of frontend and backend work

## Core Features We Plan to Implement
1. Product browsing with multi-currency display
2. Shopping cart management
3. Price comparison simulation
4. Payment simulation (no real payment)
5. Logistics tracking simulation
6. User account management
